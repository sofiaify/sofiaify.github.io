I started with analog photography in 2023, when I found a new lens through which to see the world. I fell in love with the entire process. From deciding the exact moment to take a picture, to developing the film and mastering the many techniques along the way.

To this day I keep shooting with my old camera from the 1970s, which accompanies me on all of my travels as I explore different cultures and landscapes.
