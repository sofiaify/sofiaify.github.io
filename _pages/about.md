My name is Sofia Fuentes. I'm 29 years old and graduated as an Industrial Engineer from the Pontificia Universidad Catolica de Chile. I'm a self-taught artist who loves painting, design and experimenting across many mediums.

## How I work

My works are a mix between:

- **Analog photography:** shot on 35 mm film with a camera from the 70s that travels everywhere with me.
- **Watercolor:**  a technique I've explored since 2020 to relax and create.
- **Digital art:**  a fast, accessible way to create modern pieces and bring in design elements.
- **Various:**  murals, 3D design and collaborations that take my work off the page and into real spaces.

## A little more

I’ve been a big fan of art since I was a kid. Throughout my life, I’ve connected with different forms of art, including oil painting, drawing, and mosaic. In 2020, during the pandemic, I began exploring watercolors and discovered a way of painting that helps me slow down and connect with myself. A few years later, I fell in love with analog photography and the whole process behind it: shooting, developing, and scanning.

More recently, I’ve embraced digital art, transforming spaces into places with a style of their own. Today, I also help other artists and brands tell their stories through branding and design.
