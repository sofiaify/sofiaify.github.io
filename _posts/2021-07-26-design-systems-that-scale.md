A design system is a set of interconnected patterns and shared practices coherently organised to aid in digital product design and development.

## Start with tokens

Design tokens are the single source of truth for colour, spacing, and typography. They keep your product consistent across platforms.

## Document everything

A component is only as useful as its documentation. Provide clear usage guidelines, do's and don'ts, and live examples.

- Establish naming conventions
- Version your components
- Automate accessibility checks
