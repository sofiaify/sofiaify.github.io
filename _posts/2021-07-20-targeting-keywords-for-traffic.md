Keyword research is the foundation of any successful SEO strategy. It helps you understand what your audience is searching for and how to reach them.

## Find the right keywords

Focus on intent, not just volume. Long-tail keywords often convert better because they match specific needs.

## Create valuable content

Search engines reward content that genuinely answers a user's question. Write for people first, search engines second.

- Research search intent
- Analyse the competition
- Measure and iterate
