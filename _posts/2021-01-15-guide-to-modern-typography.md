Typography is one of the most powerful tools in a designer's toolkit. The right typeface sets the tone before a single word is read.

## Pairing typefaces

A reliable approach is to pair a characterful display face with a neutral, highly readable body face.

## Establish a scale

A consistent type scale creates rhythm and hierarchy throughout your interface.

- Limit yourself to two or three typefaces
- Respect line length and line height
- Test across devices
