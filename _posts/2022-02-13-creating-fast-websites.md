Making sure your website runs fast and loads quickly is a fundamental part of the web design and SEO process. Page speed affects everything from user experience to search rankings.

## Optimise your images

Large, uncompressed images are the most common cause of slow websites. Serve appropriately sized images in modern formats like WebP.

> Performance is a feature, not an afterthought.

## Minimise render blocking resources

Defer non-critical JavaScript and inline critical CSS so the browser can paint meaningful content as quickly as possible.

- Compress and cache static assets
- Use a content delivery network
- Lazy-load offscreen images
