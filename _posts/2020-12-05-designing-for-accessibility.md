Accessibility should not be an afterthought bolted on at the end of a project. When considered from the start, it improves the experience for all users.

## Semantic markup

Using the right HTML element for the job gives assistive technology the context it needs.

> The web is fundamentally designed to work for all people.

## Colour and contrast

Ensure text has sufficient contrast against its background and never rely on colour alone to convey meaning.

- Support keyboard navigation
- Provide descriptive alt text
- Respect reduced motion preferences
